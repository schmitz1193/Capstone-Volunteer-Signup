VOLUNTEER SIGN-UP

A sign-up app for volunteers and volunteer coordinators

Stephanie Krause
January 5, 2016

Project Goals:

  • Volunteers can view a volunteer opportunity and sign up
  • Volunteers can view what they have signed up for
  • Volunteer coordinator can add volunteer opportunities to the calendar
  • Volunteer coordinator can view who has volunteered

Technologies Used:

  • AngularJS
  • Bootstrap
  • Arshaw FullCalendar
  • Moment.js
